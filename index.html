About Us


Our research group is committed to exploring advanced 3D vision and artificial intelligence technologies in the field of sports. Through technological innovation and practical application, we provide theoretical support and technical guarantee for the intelligent transformation of the sports field, and help the digital transformation of the physical education and national fitness. By integrating motion capture systems, virtual reality, augmented reality, and visualization analysis technologies, we not only provides invaluable insights for athletes and coaches but also contributes significantly to academic research in proactive health and sports science.
