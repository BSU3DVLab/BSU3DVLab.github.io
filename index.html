About us


The Beijing Sports University (BSU) 3DV Lab stands at the forefront of technological innovation in the field of sports science, specializing in three-dimensional visualization technologies. As a key facility within one of China's premier sports education institutions, the 3DV Lab harnesses cutting-edge 3D imaging and analysis tools to study biomechanics, sports techniques, and the physical movements of athletes in unprecedented detail. Our lab supports a wide range of research and educational initiatives, aiming to enhance athletic performance, prevent injuries, and contribute to the development of new training methodologies. By integrating virtual reality (VR), motion capture systems, and advanced simulation technologies, the BSU 3DV Lab not only provides invaluable insights for athletes and coaches but also contributes significantly to academic research in sports science.
